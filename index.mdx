      You'll need a comma four device to run sunnypilot. The comma four provides the computing power and sensor interfaces required for driver assistance.
      
      <Note>
        Additional hardware like a harness box and mount are required. See [hardware requirements](/vehicles/hardware-requirements) for details.
      </Note>
    </Step>
    
    <Step title="Install sunnypilot">
      Follow the installation instructions to flash sunnypilot onto your comma four device. Choose from stable, release candidate, or development branches based on your needs.
      
      ```bash
      # Installation URL format
      https://installer.comma.ai/username/branch
      ```
      
      Check out our [installation guide](/installation) for step-by-step instructions.
    </Step>
    
    <Step title="Configure and Drive">
      Mount the device, connect to your vehicle's OBD-C port, and configure your preferences. sunnypilot will guide you through the initial setup on first boot.
      
      Review [safety guidelines](/guides/safety) before your first drive with sunnypilot engaged.
    </Step>
  </Steps>
