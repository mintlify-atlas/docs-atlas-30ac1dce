## Forum Structure

The community forum is organized into several categories:

### Announcements
Official announcements about releases, features, and important updates from the sunnypilot team.

### General Discussion
Discuss general topics related to sunnypilot, share your experiences, and connect with other users.

### Support
Get help with installation, troubleshooting, and technical issues. Community members and developers are here to assist.

### Feature Requests
Suggest new features and improvements. Your feedback directly influences development priorities.

### Car-Specific Discussions
Find dedicated threads for specific makes and models to discuss car-specific tuning and experiences.

## Community Guidelines

<Warning>
  **Safety First**: sunnypilot is an advanced driver assistance system. Always follow safety guidelines and local laws. You are responsible for your vehicle at all times.
</Warning>

When participating in the forum:

- Be respectful and constructive
- Search before posting to avoid duplicates
- Provide detailed information when asking for help (car model, sunnypilot version, branch, etc.)
- Share logs and relevant data when reporting issues
- Follow the forum rules and moderator guidance

## Getting Help

When you need support:

1. **Search first**: Your question may have already been answered
2. **Provide details**: Include your car make/model/year, sunnypilot version, and branch
3. **Share logs**: Upload relevant error logs to help diagnose issues
4. **Be patient**: Community members volunteer their time to help

<Tip>
  The forum has a powerful search feature. Try searching for your car model or specific feature to find relevant discussions.
</Tip>

## Contributing to the Community

Everyone can contribute:

- **Answer questions**: Help other users with problems you've solved
- **Share experiences**: Write about your real-world usage and tuning
- **Report issues**: Help identify bugs and areas for improvement
- **Provide feedback**: Share what's working well and what could be better
- **Create guides**: Document solutions and workflows for others

## Stay Connected

Don't miss important updates:

- Enable email notifications for categories you're interested in
- Bookmark important threads

- Check the forum regularly for new features and updates
