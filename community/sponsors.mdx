## Sponsorship Tiers

GitHub Sponsors offers multiple tiers to fit your budget and level of support:

<Info>
  **Sponsor Tier Features**: The sunnypilot UI includes sponsor tier color mapping that recognizes your contribution level
</Info>

Each tier provides different benefits:

- **Supporter Tier**: Show your appreciation with a small monthly contribution
- **Enthusiast Tier**: Get early access to new features and updates
- **Contributor Tier**: Gain influence over feature priorities and development direction
- **Premium Tier**: Maximum benefits including priority support and exclusive access

## How Your Support Helps

sunnypilot is maintained by dedicated developers who invest significant time and resources:

### Development Time
- Implementing new features from the community
- Maintaining code quality and safety standards
- Testing across 300+ supported car models
- Reviewing and integrating community contributions

### Infrastructure Costs
- Server hosting for sunnylink services
- CI/CD pipeline and automated testing
- Model storage and distribution
- Community forum hosting

### Testing & Hardware
- Test vehicles and equipment
- comma devices for development
- Specialized hardware for car-specific features

## Special Features for Sponsors

### sunnylink Integration

Sponsors gain access to enhanced sunnylink features:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Settings Backup" icon="cloud-arrow-up">
    Remotely back up and restore your sunnypilot settings with AES encryption
  </Card>
  <Card title="Remote Management" icon="mobile">
    Remote settings management capabilities (upcoming feature)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Secure Storage" icon="lock">
    End-to-end encrypted storage of your configuration
  </Card>
  <Card title="Multi-Device Sync" icon="rotate">
    Easily transfer settings between devices
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Warning>
  **Security Note**: Settings are encrypted on-device using AES encryption derived from your device's RSA private key, transmitted securely via HTTPS, and stored encrypted on sunnylink servers.
</Warning>

### Early Access Programs

As noted in the changelog, sponsors receive:

- Early alpha access to features like sunnylink Config/Settings Backup
- Priority testing of new driving models
- Beta access to experimental features
- Exclusive development updates
