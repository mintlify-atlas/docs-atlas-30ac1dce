## Before Your First Drive

Before engaging sunnypilot for the first time, complete these essential preparation steps to ensure a safe and successful experience.

<Warning>
  **Driver Responsibility**: sunnypilot is a driver assistance system. You must remain alert and ready to take control at all times. Read the [safety guidelines](/guides/safety) before proceeding.
</Warning>

### Pre-Drive Checklist

<Steps>
  <Step title="Verify Device Installation">
    Ensure your comma device is properly installed and connected:
    
    - Device is securely mounted to windshield
    - OBD-C cable is firmly connected to the vehicle's diagnostic port
    - Cameras have clear, unobstructed views (road-facing and driver-facing)
    - Device powers on when the vehicle is started
    
    <Tip>
      The device should automatically boot when you turn on your vehicle. If it doesn't, check the OBD-C connection.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete Device Calibration">
    sunnypilot requires camera calibration before first use:
    
    - Drive above **20 mph (32 km/h)** on a straight road
    - Keep the road markings clearly visible
    - Calibration progress displays on screen: `Calibrating: X%`
    - Typically completes within 1-2 miles of highway driving
    
    <Info>
      If you see "Recalibrating" instead of "Calibrating," the device detected a change in mounting position and is re-calibrating automatically.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Check System Status">
    Before engaging, verify:
    
    - No red alerts or warnings on screen
    - Green engagement button is available
    - Driver monitoring camera can see your face
    - GPS signal is acquired (optional for first drive, but recommended)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Controls">
    Familiarize yourself with engagement controls:
    
    **Engagement Methods:**
    - Press **SET** or **RESUME** button on steering wheel
    - For MADS users: Engagement behavior depends on your MADS configuration
    
    **Disengagement Methods:**
    - Press **CANCEL** button on steering wheel
    - Press brake pedal firmly
    - Turn the steering wheel to override (lateral control pauses)
    - Press accelerator (if "Disengage on Accelerator Pedal" is enabled)
    
    <Warning>
      **Always** be ready to take control by braking or steering. These override actions work instantly, even if sunnypilot is engaged.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Mounting Your Device

Proper mounting is critical for sunnypilot performance and safety.

### Recommended Mount Position

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Optimal Placement" icon="location-dot">
    - Behind the rearview mirror (centered)
    - As close to windshield as possible
    - Cameras have unobstructed forward and driver views
    - Does not interfere with airbag deployment zones
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="What to Avoid" icon="triangle-exclamation">
    - Tinted windshield areas that block cameras
    - Positions where wipers obstruct the camera
    - Too low (limits forward visibility)
    - Off-center positions (affects calibration)
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Mounting Tips

1. **Clean the windshield** thoroughly before applying the mount
2. **Position carefully** - once attached, repositioning requires recalibration
3. **Test the view** - ensure road markings are visible in the camera preview
4. **Secure cables** - route the OBD-C cable neatly to avoid interference

<Tip>
  Use the onboard camera preview in **Settings → Device** to verify your camera angles before driving.
</Tip>

## First Engagement

Follow these steps for a controlled first engagement:

### Choose the Right Conditions

For your first drive with sunnypilot engaged, select:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Road Conditions">
    - **Highway or major road** with clear lane markings
    - **Good weather** - avoid rain, fog, or snow for first attempt
    - **Light traffic** - gives you time to familiarize yourself
    - **Daylight** - better visibility for you and the cameras
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Speed Recommendations">
    Start at moderate speeds:
    - Minimum engagement speed varies by vehicle (typically 15-25 mph)
    - Begin around **45-55 mph** on highway
    - Increase confidence before trying stop-and-go traffic
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Engagement Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Accelerate to Cruising Speed">
    Bring your vehicle up to your desired speed using the accelerator pedal.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Engage Cruise Control">
    Press the **SET** button on your steering wheel. You should:
    
    - Hear a chime (depending on your vehicle)
    - See the green path appear on screen
    - Feel sunnypilot taking control of steering and/or speed
    - See engagement status in the UI
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Monitor System Behavior">
    During your first minutes engaged:
    
    - Keep hands lightly on the wheel (don't grip too tightly)
    - Watch the path prediction on screen
    - Observe how sunnypilot handles curves and lane keeping
    - Check driver monitoring alerts - keep eyes on road
    
    <Note>
      sunnypilot includes driver monitoring. If you look away from the road, you'll receive "Pay Attention" warnings.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Practice Disengagement">
    Test the override controls:
    
    - Lightly brake to disengage
    - Re-engage with **RESUME** button
    - Try steering override (lateral control pauses)
    - Press **CANCEL** button to fully disengage
    
    Practice these until they become second nature.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What to Expect

### Normal Behavior

During normal operation, you should observe:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Lateral Control">
    **Steering Assistance:**
    - Smooth, gentle steering inputs to keep centered in lane
    - Slight adjustments as road curves
    - May feel different from stock systems (often smoother)
    - Blue path indicates laneline mode (if using Dynamic Lane Profile)
    - Rainbow path available (enable in Visuals settings)
    
    **What's Normal:**
    - Slight wandering while learning your car (first few drives)
    - Conservative positioning in curves
    - May ping-pong slightly on poorly marked roads
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Longitudinal Control">
    **Speed & Following:**
    - Maintains set cruise speed when no lead vehicle
    - Follows lead vehicle at safe distance (adjustable via Driving Personality)
    - Smooth acceleration and braking (personality-dependent)
    - Stop-and-go capability on supported vehicles
    
    **Driving Personality Options:**
    - **Chill** (openpilot ACC): Stock-like behavior, comfortable following
    - **Standard**: Balanced performance
    - **Aggressive**: Tighter following, quicker responses
    - **Maniac**: Most aggressive (use with caution)
    
    <Tip>
      Adjust Driving Personality with the **DISTANCE** or **FOLLOW DISTANCE** button on your steering wheel (on supported vehicles).
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Visual Indicators">
    **On-Screen Elements:**
    - **Green path**: System engaged and active
    - **Orange path**: Driver steering override detected
    - **Lead vehicle indicator**: Shows detected car ahead with distance/speed
    - **Speed display**: Current speed (or true speed if enabled)
    - **Cruise speed**: Target speed setting
    - **Lane lines**: Detected road markings
    - **Turn signals**: On-screen when blinker active (if enabled)
    - **Blindspot indicators**: When vehicles detected (if enabled)
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Audio Alerts">
    **Chimes and Warnings:**
    - **Engagement chime**: Confirms activation (vehicle-dependent)
    - **"Pay Attention"**: Driver monitoring alert - refocus on road
    - **Disengagement chime**: System has disengaged
    - **Forward Collision Warning**: Hard chime - risk of collision
    - **Lane Departure Warning**: Alert when crossing lane lines while disengaged
    - **Green Light Chime**: Traffic light ahead turned green (if enabled)
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### First Drive Tips

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Do" icon="circle-check">
    - Keep hands on or near the wheel at all times
    - Monitor system behavior and path prediction
    - Practice engagement and disengagement frequently
    - Start on familiar roads
    - Read driver monitoring prompts and respond appropriately
    - Take control anytime you feel uncertain
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Don't" icon="circle-xmark">
    - Don't assume sunnypilot will handle every situation
    - Don't use your phone or look away from the road
    - Don't engage in heavy traffic on your first attempt
    - Don't test limits - be conservative initially
    - Don't ignore alerts or warnings
    - Don't use in construction zones until experienced
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Common First-Time Scenarios

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="System Won't Engage" icon="circle-exclamation">
    **Possible Reasons:**
    - Speed below minimum engagement threshold (check: `Drive above X mph to engage`)
    - Calibration not complete (check: `Calibrating: X%`)
    - Safety critical alert active (check screen for red/orange alerts)
    - Brake pedal pressed
    - Parking brake engaged
    - Door open
    - Seatbelt not fastened (vehicle-dependent)
    
    **Solution:** Address the displayed alert, ensure you're above minimum speed, and try again.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Immediate Disengagement" icon="circle-exclamation">
    **Possible Causes:**
    - Brake pedal tap
    - Sudden steering input
    - Speed dropped below minimum
    - CAN bus error or communication issue
    - Safety violation detected
    
    **Solution:** Check for alerts on screen. If it's a CAN error, pull over, turn off vehicle for 30 seconds, restart, and try again.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Steering Feels Too Aggressive/Timid" icon="circle-exclamation">
    This is normal during the first few drives. sunnypilot uses live parameter tuning that adapts to your vehicle over time.
    
    **What to do:**
    - Continue driving for 15-20 minutes to allow paramsd to learn
    - Check lateral tuning in **Settings → Controls → Lateral Control**
    - Consider Neural Network Lateral Control (NNLC) if your vehicle is supported
    - Adjust torque settings if available for your platform
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Driver Monitoring Keeps Alerting" icon="circle-exclamation">
    sunnypilot requires you to keep your eyes on the road at all times.
    
    **Tips to avoid alerts:**
    - Look at the road, not the device screen
    - Ensure driver camera can see your face (not blocked by sunglasses/hat)
    - Sit in a natural position facing forward
    - Don't look down at your lap or phone
    
    <Warning>
      **Do not** attempt to disable or bypass driver monitoring. It's a critical safety feature.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Building Confidence

As you gain experience:

- **Gradually expand conditions**: Try different weather, traffic, and road types
- **Practice interventions**: Regular disengagement practice builds muscle memory
- **Learn system limits**: Understand when to take control proactively
- **Join the community**: Share experiences and learn from others at [community.sunnypilot.ai](https://community.sunnypilot.ai)

<Note>
  Remember: sunnypilot is **always** in beta. Even experienced users must remain vigilant and ready to intervene.
</Note>
