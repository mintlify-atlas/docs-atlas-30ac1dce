sunnypilot supports over 336 vehicles from 26+ manufacturers. A supported vehicle is one that works when you install a comma device with sunnypilot. All supported cars provide an enhanced driving experience with modified assist behaviors while maintaining compliance with safety rules.

<Note>
Supported vehicles reference the US market unless otherwise specified.
</Note>

## What Makes a Car Supported?

For a car to be fully supported by sunnypilot, it must have:

- **Adaptive Cruise Control (ACC)** - Maintains distance from vehicles ahead
- **Lane Keeping/Centering System** - Provides steering assistance
- **Electronic steering, gas, and brake interfaces** - Required for sunnypilot control

These features typically started appearing in vehicles around 2016. Note that manufacturers use different marketing names for these features:

- Hyundai/Kia: "Smart Cruise Control (SCC)"
- Honda: "Honda Sensing"
- Toyota/Lexus: "Toyota Safety Sense (TSS)" or "Lexus Safety System+"
- Ford: "Co-Pilot360"
- GM: "Safety Package" or "Adaptive Cruise Control Package"

## Don't See Your Car?

<Info>
sunnypilot can support many more cars than it currently does. If your car has ACC and LKAS/LCA, there's a good chance it can be supported! Most new car support comes from users like you.
</Info>

### Community Supported Models

Although they're not upstream, the community has sunnypilot running on other makes and models. Check the [Community Forum](https://community.sunnypilot.ai/) for more information about community-supported vehicles.

### How to Add Your Car

If your car has the required features but isn't listed, you can help add support! Visit the sunnypilot GitHub repository or community forum to learn more about the car porting process.
